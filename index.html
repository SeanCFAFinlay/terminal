The operating principle

What it is

Your rules. Decided before the trade.

Most traders lose because they change their mind mid-trade. Terminal doesn’t. You set the logic in advance, and it follows through — every single time.

Rules set in advance

Your trading logic is locked before the market opens. No last-minute changes, no emotional overrides. The rules run as written.

Built-in safety checks

If any condition isn’t met, the terminal skips the trade. Position size is calculated from your risk budget — not from how confident you feel.

Every decision logged

Taken or skipped — every trade decision is recorded with the reason why. Go back to any day and see exactly what happened and why.

It learns with you

Gets sharper from real results. Never from guesswork.

Every trade — taken or skipped — becomes data. Terminal replays them against real market conditions, tracks what actually worked, and updates its logic. But it can never break the safety rules you set.

01

Record every trade

Every decision is stored — the setup, the conditions, and the reasoning behind it.

02

Replay against real data

Trades are tested against actual market conditions — real spreads, real slippage, not a perfect simulation.

03

Track what works

The system measures which patterns deliver and which don’t, based on real outcomes over time.

04

Update and improve

Changes are reviewed, versioned, and shipped to your build as a tracked update you can inspect.

Hard limits it can never cross
  • It cannot skip a safety check.
  • It cannot risk more than you allow.
  • It cannot trade something it doesn’t have a profile for.
  • It cannot make up data or override your broker’s rules.

One engine, every market

Same logic. Different markets.

The same decision engine runs across every market. What changes is the profile for each asset — its rules, its behavior, its context.

Forex & gold

Watches yields, the dollar, and risk sentiment for context. Uses correlation as a filter, not a signal.

Crypto, 24/7

Always on. Weekend risk checks, exchange spread monitoring, and funding context. No blind spots when markets never close.

Equities & ETFs

Sector context, earnings-window awareness, and rate sensitivity. Tracks trends and provides context for your decisions.

How it runs

Mode 02

Mode 03
